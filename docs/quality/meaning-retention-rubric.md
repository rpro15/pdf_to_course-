# Meaning Retention Rubric v1.0

## Scoring Bands
- **90-100**: Core ideas and constraints are preserved with no factual drift.
- **70-89**: Main ideas are preserved, minor detail loss acceptable.
- **50-69**: Partial retention; missing context or unclear explanations.
- **0-49**: Meaning is significantly distorted or absent.

## Evaluation Dimensions
1. Concept coverage (core ideas preserved)
2. Constraint preservation (rules, limits, conditions)
3. Terminology fidelity (domain terms retained correctly)
4. Logical coherence (cause/effect and sequence preserved)

## Review Trigger
- Module quality score below 70 is flagged as **review required**.
